ALWAYS WATCH YOUR FIRES

Fires have a tendency to spread quickly especially if left unattended. Fires have a tendency to spread at random and leaving fires by themselves would likely burn something while you are distracted. So be sure to always watch your fires as much as you watch your homework schedule!

PUT OUT YOUR FFIREPLACE WHEN NOT IN USE

Similar to not leaving fires unattended, fire places are the fires biggest friend. Fireplaces tend to be placed in living rooms where many stuff such as cotton based items, floor mats, or even rugs are abundant. Fire tends to spark and crackle making the items mention to be caught on fire. So don't forget to either watch the fireplace always or just put it out when not in use.

CLEAN YOUR CLUTTER

Fire dosnt spread on itself, it needs a little help. Stuff such as furniture, woods, and other similar items can help make fires bigger. A cluttered house means a bigger fire, so why not try cleaning up as it helps the eyes and will make your home safer!

CHANGE YOUR FILTERS

Lint is basically the love of fire. Lint can be easily ignited even from heat as it is very flammable and can come in many amounts. Whats even ironic is that there are filters that catch lint in your heating systems so why not try checking up on them to see if you need to clean it out?

TURN OFF HEATED APPLIANCES WHEN UNATTENDED

As mentioned previously, many heated items such as dryers and heating systems have lint trapping filters to them. Leaving these items on for long amounts of times may cause a spark and a fire to appear. Make sure to never forget to turn them off!
